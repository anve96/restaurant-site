Our Story

Three generations of family, passion, and homemade Italian-American cooking.

Est. 1987

From Nonna's Kitchen to Your Table

La Famiglia was born in 1987 when Rosaria and Giuseppe Moretti opened a small trattoria in Brooklyn, New York. Armed with recipes handed down from their mothers and grandmothers back in Naples, they wanted to recreate the warmth of an Italian Sunday dinner for everyone who walked through their door.

Today, their grandchildren run the restaurant with the same love and dedication. Every sauce is still made from scratch, every pasta is hand-rolled, and every guest is treated like family.
